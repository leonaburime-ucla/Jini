# interface-design:critique

Critique a completed interface for craft, then rebuild the parts that defaulted.

## Review Pass

Look for:

- flat or monotone composition
- weak rhythm or spacing
- unclear hierarchy
- generic surfaces or border/shadow choices
- missing interactive states
- structure held together with layout hacks

## Process

1. Review the output as a design lead would.
2. Identify where the work defaulted instead of deciding.
3. Rebuild those parts from the decision, not from a patch.
4. Keep the critique focused on the visible interface.
